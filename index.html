Nissan Skyline GT-R R32

The R32 is the eight generation of the skyline. After May 1989, and it's 16 years of non-production, it was brought back into the market. Out of all the sedans in the world, this car has some of the best perfomances of all time. This car is flashy, quick, and very fun to mod. The perfect car for you!

"Godzilla"

This car is commonly referred to as "Godzilla". It's performance exceeds all other cars and therefore, the community began to nickname it, "Godzilla". This car is also referred to as "The Monster From Japan". The nicknames definetely fit for the insane perfomance this car has.

Specifications & Performance

Engine

2.6l, 276hp, 271 t

Transmission

5 speed manual

Drivetrain

ATTESA E-TS AWD system, Super-HICAS 4-wheel steering

Perfomance Stats

0-60mph, quarter mile, top speed.

Design & Features

Exterior

Agressive stance, widebody, lightweight design, factory 16-inch allow wheels, function aero (spoiler, vents, and boxy JDM styling)

Interior

Sporty bucket seats with GT-R branding, digital boost gauge and unique cluster layout, lightweight materials focused on performance.

Motorsports legacy

Group A Racing

Dominated Japanese touring Car Championships (JTCC), won the 1991 & 1992 Bathurst 100 in Australia, banned from racing due to being too dominant

Drag & Tuning Culture

Easy to tune, (1000+ hp possible w/ mods, popular for street racing, drifting, and time attack.

variants & Special editions

R32 GT-R Nismo

Lightweight, better aerodynamics

R32 GT-R V-Spec & V-Spec II

Upgraded brakes, bigger wheels

R32 GT-R N1

Built for racing, stripped-down version

Tuning & Modifications

Common Mods

Bigger turbochargers, ECU tuning for better fuel and ignition maps, aftermarket axhaust and intake for more airflow, bigger intercoolers to prevent heat soak.

Notable tuners

HKS, Mines, Tomei, blitz, Top Secret

Fun Facts

The R32 rev limiter is at 8,000 RPM, but the RB26 can handle much more, Nissan underreported horsepower due to Japan's "Gentlemen's Agreement.", It was the first Skyline GT-R to use AWD and twin turbos.
